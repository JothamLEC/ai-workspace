# Shortcomings of Traditional KYC

## Overview

Traditional KYC (Know Your Customer) processes come in two main types: **Manual KYC** and **Video KYC**. Both have significant limitations compared to modern eKYC solutions.

## Manual KYC

### Limitations

#### 1. Physical Presence Required
**Problem:** Manual KYC requires customers to visit physical branches.

**Impact:**
- Inconvenient for customers
- Time-consuming travel
- Limited to business hours
- Geographic barriers
- Queue waiting times
- Not accessible 24/7

**Solution:** [EMAS eKYC](../products/emas-ekyc.md) enables remote verification from anywhere, anytime.

#### 2. Manual Processing
**Problem:** Human-driven verification process.

**Impact:**
- Prone to human error
- Inconsistent verification standards
- Delays in processing
- Subjective decision-making
- Requires extensive training
- Variable quality control

**Solution:** Automated AI-powered verification through [OkayDoc](../modules/okaydoc.md) and [OkayFace](../modules/okayface.md) provides consistent, accurate results.

#### 3. Paper-Based System
**Problem:** Relies on physical documents and storage.

**Impact:**
- Difficult to manage at scale
- Security risks (theft, loss, damage)
- Large storage space requirements
- Hard to search and retrieve
- Environmental impact
- Compliance challenges

**Solution:** Digital storage in [OkayDB](../modules/okaydb.md) with encrypted, searchable, and compliant data management.

#### 4. High Costs
**Problem:** Expensive operational requirements.

**Cost Factors:**
- Physical infrastructure (branches, offices)
- Staffing (receptionists, verifiers, data entry)
- Storage facilities
- Security measures
- Training programs
- Overhead expenses

**Solution:** [EMAS eKYC](../products/emas-ekyc.md) drastically reduces costs through automation and eliminates need for physical infrastructure.

## Video KYC

### Limitations

#### 1. Real-Time Interaction Required
**Problem:** Requires live connection with KYC officer.

**Impact:**
- Time-consuming for both parties
- Inconvenient scheduling
- Customer must be available at specific time
- Officer must be dedicated during call
- Language barriers possible
- Communication difficulties

**Solution:** [EMAS eKYC](../products/emas-ekyc.md) provides asynchronous verification without real-time interaction needed.

#### 2. Limited Availability
**Problem:** May not be available 24/7.

**Impact:**
- Restricted to business hours
- Limited by timezone differences
- Weekend/holiday limitations
- Can't serve global customers effectively
- Queue times during peak hours
- Scaling challenges

**Solution:** Fully automated [EMAS eKYC](../products/emas-ekyc.md) operates 24/7/365 without human limitations.

#### 3. Scalability Issues
**Problem:** Requires significant human resources.

**Impact:**
- Can't handle large customer volumes
- Need to hire more staff to scale
- Training time for new staff
- Quality inconsistency across officers
- High operational costs
- Limited to available human capacity

**Solution:** [EMAS eKYC](../products/emas-ekyc.md) scales infinitely without additional human resources.

#### 4. Security Concerns
**Problem:** Vulnerable to fraud and impersonation.

**Security Risks:**
- **Deepfake technology** - AI-generated videos can mimic real people
- **Video manipulation** - Pre-recorded or edited videos
- **Social engineering** - Fraudsters can deceive human verifiers
- **Inconsistent verification** - Human judgment varies
- **Screen sharing risks** - Potential data exposure

**Solution:**
- [OkayLive](../modules/okaylive.md) - Passive liveness detection prevents spoofing
- [OkayDoc](../modules/okaydoc.md) - ~20 security checks detect fake documents
- [OkayFace](../modules/okayface.md) - 99.50%+ accuracy with 0.000001 false match rate
