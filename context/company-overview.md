# Innov8tif Company Overview

## About the Company

Innov8tif is a technology company specializing in digital identity verification and authentication solutions. The company provides Electronic Know Your Customer (eKYC) systems and digital identity assurance platforms primarily for businesses in Asia, particularly in the ASEAN region.

## Core Products

### EMAS eKYC
A proprietary digital identity verification system that enables businesses to verify customer identities remotely without human intervention. EMAS eKYC consists of five integrated modules working together to provide comprehensive identity verification.

**Learn more:** [EMAS eKYC](./products/emas-ekyc.md)

### EMAS CIDA
A flexible, customizable framework that allows digital businesses to scale their Assurance, Authentication, and Authorization (3A) requirements. Built on four layers: Portal Access, Channel, Features, and Data.

**Learn more:** [EMAS CIDA](./products/emas-cida.md)

## Market Position

Innov8tif positions itself as a leader in digital identity verification in the ASEAN region, offering the most comprehensive anti-fraud ID solution in the market with approximately 20 security checks.

### Key Differentiators
- **Most comprehensive solution in ASEAN** - ~20 security checks for anti-fraud
- **Passive liveness detection** - Improved user experience without awkward movements
- **High accuracy** - 99.50%+ facial recognition accuracy with 0.000001 false match rate
- **Complete automation** - No human touchpoints required
- **Regional expertise** - Focus on ASEAN markets with localized solutions
- **Modular approach** - Customizable based on business needs

1. **Banking & Finance** - Digital banking, customer onboarding, transaction validation
