# Financial Risk Checks

## Overview

A financial risk check involves assessing the risk of onboarding an individual or entity that may pose a liability to the business. It is a crucial part of the Know Your Customer (KYC) and Anti-Money Laundering (AML) compliance processes.

## What It Includes

Beyond verifying the identity of the customer, financial risk checks also include:
- Assessing creditworthiness
- Evaluating potential to engage in financial crimes
- Checking for money laundering connections
- Screening for terrorist financing links

## Why Is It Important?

### Regulatory Compliance
- Maintaining integrity of the financial system
- Preventing illegal activities (money laundering, terrorist financing)
- Ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements
- Avoiding penalties and fines

### Business Protection
- Protecting businesses from committing financial crimes
- Preventing reputational damage
- Mitigating financial risks associated with customers
- Reducing exposure to criminal activities

### Risk Management
- Evaluating customer creditworthiness
- Assessing transaction risk levels
- Identifying high-risk individuals or entities
- Preventing fraud and financial losses
